### Oracle Tables Written By The System
1. `PIO_AML_SCENARIO` вҖ” scenario header
2. `PIO_AML_RULES` вҖ” rule definition (period, type)
3. `PIO_AML_SCENARIO_RULES` вҖ” scenario-rule linkage
4. `PIO_AML_RULES_DETAILS` вҖ” individual conditions (batch insert)

### Procedure Executed By Validator
`FILL_PIO_AML_CUSTOMERS` вҶ’ alerts land in `PIO_AML_CUSTOMERS` вҶ’ filtered by `AML_SCENARIO_CODE`
